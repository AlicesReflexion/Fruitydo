Wait, so what is this?

Fruitydo is a productivity app that merges all the best features of a to do list, a diary, and a calendar, so you can get more done, and keep track of your progress.

To start by creating a goal with a name, for example, "Learn Japanese." Goals can have due dates, sub-tasks, and reappear on certain days of the week. When you create your goal, you're given a Progress Calendar. The progress calendar gives you a personal diary entry for each date, letting you write down your achievements on that date. For example, your "Learn Japanese" progress calendar might contain an entry every day you learn a new word.
